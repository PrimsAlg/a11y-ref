General Accessibility

This section deals with some accessibility topics that are either general and apply to multiple file formats or are other relevant topics that aren't specific to any file format.

  • Writing alt text discusses good practices when writing alt text for a variety of different types of content.
  • Headings discusses how headings are organized and why they are important.
  • Color addresses the importance of things like color contrast and designing for color blindness
  • Fonts contains information on font size and families.
  • Hyperlinks discusses how hyperlinks should be handled in documents.
  • Software comparison shows the differences between different versions of office that may impact the accessibility of documents that are produced in them.
  • Lists explains the structure of lists and the appropriate use of unordered and ordered lists.
